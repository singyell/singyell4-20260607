Answers To Your Doubts

Frequently Answered Specs

Singyell uses a customized setup centering around Blender Cycles, Cinema 4D, Redshift, and Unreal Engine 5. For procedural work, Houdini particles are exported as high-fidelity render assets.

Typically, simple reference sketch files, material blueprints, or specific CAD files (.STEP, .OBJ, .FBX) are perfect. If starting completely from imagination, mood boards or a concept text outline are sufficient.

A standalone high-definition visual usually takes 3 to 7 working days. Fully articulated CGI motion commercials range from 2 to 4 weeks depending on procedural particle assets and camera angles.
